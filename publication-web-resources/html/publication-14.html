A Poetic Approach to Climate Change

Landscapes become a part of our souls when we climb their mountains and trees, delight at the rainbows of their wildflowers, or hear the stories of ancient groves and endless seas of grass. When they change, we feel a deep sorrow. After everything changes, the Solastalgia Sanctuaries will tell the story of a landscape that once was.

Through sky and earth, with underwater labyrinths and peaceful groves, these gardens employ strategies of spatial manipulation and optical illusion to weave a story of landscape, love, loss, memory, and our journey through an unpredictable future, overwhelming present, and irrevocable past.

Golden Garden

Northwestern Forested Mountain Ecoregion

Golden, BC

A Poetic Approach to Climate Change

This project drew from the strengths of both digital modelling tools and hand crafting techniques.

The main garden design was modelled fully in Rhino and used to CNC a physical model of complex topography. Digital line-work of the perspectives, plan, and section were used to laser-cut pieces from various cardstocks, holographic and iridescent paper, and vellums stained with alcohol inks and speckled with shimmer paints. Cut-outs were arranged and glued by hand.

For our gallery display, I crafted Grow-Your-Own Golden Garden seed boxes based on the garden ecosystem types and planting palettes.
