A Poetic Approach to Climate Change

A detailed site analysis led to two very different concept proposals.

The first, “Skyline Circuits”, followed the existing design’s use of geometric shapes, with additional space created through vertical seating elements in the form of blocky walls. To address the darkness, several “Lumen Blumen” solar panel sculptures would rise above the shadows and collect energy for extensive ground lighting styled as roots/circuit boards.

The second design, “Ebbs and Eddies” was chosen for its soft, organic forms and concept of working with the darkness rather than against it.
