A design based on bad design alone will generally be, well, bad. So while the anti-design helps create a kind of force-field around fragile naturalization areas, there are also plenty of instances of good design in the surrounding areas to lure people away and also to simply provide a good place to enjoy.

The saltwater pool is the biggest draw, with artificial sand surfaces, natural plantings, convenient amenities, and safe play areas. The site there also features a pavilion, bandshell, dog park, skate park, picnic tables, athletic fields, labyrinths, bird blinds, formal gardens, forest trails, and bicycle rentals.
