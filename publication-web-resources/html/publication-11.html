A Poetic Approach to Climate Change

Inviting the Light

A Quiet Beckoning

22

When daylighting creeks, much more than water is revealed.

The gentle allure of an open walking path calls for aimless wanderings with an open mind.

A descent into the lush humidity of a thriving ecosystem excites the curiosity of life around us.

The contracted darkness emerging into the sunshine of open skies brings comfort and a desire to connect.

A rambling path allowed to run where it may invites the spirit of an open heart, the spark of wonder, and a playfulness that helps us both to enjoy life and build resilient community ties.
