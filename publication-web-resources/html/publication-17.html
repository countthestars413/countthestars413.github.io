Avant-Garden

A Poetic Approach to Climate Change

While perhaps the most humble in appearance, establishing this graduate student group has been my proudest achievement from my time at the University of Calgary.

Inspired by the Test Plot initiative of the landscape architecture firm Terremoto, the Avant-Garden began from a desire to make a difference. I made a commitment to myself that I would make Test Plots happen on the university campus, despite not having the faintest clue how to go about achieving this. It ended up being one of the greatest learning experiences of my life.
