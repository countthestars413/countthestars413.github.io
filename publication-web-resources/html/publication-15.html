The Heart of Bowness

Bowness is an old neighbourhood of Calgary; rich in history, culture, and community pride.

As a lower-income neighbourhood once controlled by the Hell’s Angels, Bowness has a distinct grittiness in its character. But it’s also an incredibly tight-knit community of resilient people fiercely proud and supportive of their neighbourhood.

A brownfield site in the heart of the BIA is nearing the end of its cleanup process, and the community needs a central gathering point. This plaza, designed with the character of people and place at the forefront, provides a place to gather, share, play, and create.
