This studio design involved the development of 99% construction drawings. We completed hardscape plans, site furnishing plans, and planting plans. The detailed hardscape plan and materials schedule is shown here.

Land F/X was used to complete these drawings and produce schedules.

Materials were carefully chosen and specified to a high level of detail. Considerations for material choice include texture, colour, local availability, urban use, longevity, accessibility, and aesthetics. All organic forms use arcs, not curves.

This LID bioretention pond is designed as a softly magical rain garden and the echo of a wetland.

The Misty Marsh evokes a silvery, shimmering marshland, with tall blue grasses mimicking cattails; thinly planted marsh weeds emerging from glossy mulch; and weeping groundcovers reminiscent of a wet meadow. Purple irises border Periwinkle Pools of wildflowers and Dark Cascades of fountain grasses flow into sleek ripplings of black groundcovers.

Mulches are layered to imitate water. Dull slate chippings line the shore, while the interior pond area has round and shiny dark stones and glass, increasing in shimmer towards the center. Stepping stone paths with soft under-lighting bolsters the illusion.
