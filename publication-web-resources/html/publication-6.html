Throughout this course, we learned how to draw construction details and write detail specifications.

Assignments explored four typical materials used in landscape construction: concrete, wood, stone, and metal. We completed drawings and specifications for concrete grade beams, timber decking, stone retaining walls, and metal railings.

This technical detail begins with a solid construction of sturdy materials and finishes off with decorative elements like the stainless steel hot rolled square bars, SPEC A-36/44W, 9.5mm x 9.5mm, and artistic samples of crystallized metals in acrylic boxes.
