Grading and stormwater management were skills used throughout my design studies.

A Rambling Path includes large-scale site re-grading to create wetland pockets and excavate the pool area. The Anti-Beach includes the re-grading of a heavily channelized river mouth to a more natural shape that could support diverse native plant communities. For other courses I designed parking lot LID features for an existing TOC and bio-swales for a traffic calming project.

This project was designed on university grounds and includes a swale, LID feature, land art feature, and parking lot design.
